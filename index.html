About Us
Making Desserts more Convenient and Delicious with Organic and Environment Friendly Products.

Inspired by the inconvenience of traditional food containers that either can't keep food at the right temperature or are too bulky to carry, Bis-Bowl was created as a solution to these problems. We believe that food should always be enjoyed at its optimal temperature, whether you're on-the-go, at the office, or at a picnic.

Give an all new refreshing experience to your customers with attractive biscuit bowls. Its tasty,nutritious, value for money, environmental and economically feasible.

We look forward to providing you with an enjoyable and convenient food experience.
